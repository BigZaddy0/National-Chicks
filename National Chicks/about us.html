Know More About Us at National Chicks

National Chicks believes in adding value to our chicks and our customers, and to this end we invest in both areas heavily. A highly competent technical team is available to ensure the quality of our product as well as assist any farmer with well founded and practical advice.
More about Technical aspects of National Chicks National Chicks has three major areas in which it operates to ensure that our chicks 🐣 can be dispatched 🚚 to customers with the least stress, as travelling distances are manageable. The areas are Camperdown, where our head office is and where 65% of the fertile hatching eggs 🥚 are produced; Boshkop (Zambia ), where the largest of the hatcheries is located and 30% of the fertile eggs are produced; and Central Province , where a new breeding facility has been established to compliment our growing hatchery.

National Chicks strive to be customer driven and are proud to have a successful record in being able to deliver on our commitments and reliably supply our customers with their orders 🚚 as placed.
